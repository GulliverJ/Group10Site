Cities across the globe are evolving. Our infrastructure has been decorated with vast, complex information networks, reading every beat of the urban heart. The only issue? Accessing this data is diffcult. Really difficult. A lack of a standard format, numerous isolated data sources, and a whole host of differing software requirements make the act of using this data in any meaningful way remarkably complicated.

The Orange Labs Sensors APIs project provides a solution, offering a common platform for hosting sensor data of all types, in a standard format. Simply sign up, register a sensor, initialise your hardware, and just switch it on to begin sharing. Weâ€™ve even got some APIs for accessing the data with ease.

Plug and play architecture


We've made it as easy as possible for you to add your sensors to our network. All you need is hardware able to send a UDP string to our server, and you're good to go.
